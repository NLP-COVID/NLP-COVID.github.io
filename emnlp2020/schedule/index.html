Presentation Schedule | NLP COVID-19 Workshop @EMNLP2020

Schedule

The NLP for COVID-19 workshop @EMNLP2020 will be held on Friday, November 20, 2020. All times on this page are reported in UK/London time.

 

There will be two main sessions, the first running from 08:00am until 11:30am UK/London time, and the second running from 04:00pm until 09:00pm UK/London time.
